Privacy at a Glance

  • Location data collected only during active work shifts

  • Access strictly restricted to authorised personnel
  • Employees may request access or deletion of personal data

1. Information We Collect

To facilitate internal operations and employee management, the Phoenix Force application collects specific categories of data during the course of your employment.

Location Data

GPS location is recorded exclusively during active work shifts to verify site attendance and coordinate logistics. We do not track location when off-shift.

Camera & Media

Camera access is utilized for daily attendance verification (facial scanning) and uploading work-related documentation or incident reports.

Files & Documents

Access to device storage is required only to attach pre-existing files, certifications, or receipts directly related to operational tasks.

Personal Information

Employee ID, name, assigned roles, and internal communications are processed to maintain operational hierarchy and task assignment.

Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous crash reports and basic usage metrics to improve application stability and performance.

2. How We Use Information

Data collected through the Phoenix Force platform is used exclusively for internal Aegis Covenant business purposes. This includes verifying shift attendance, coordinating team deployments, processing payroll based on logged hours, and maintaining a secure operational environment.

We do not use employee data for targeted advertising, nor do we sell any information to third-party data brokers.

3. Data Storage & Security

All sensitive information is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS protocols. Data at rest is secured within our enterprise AWS infrastructure, adhering to strict access controls.

Security Notice

Aegis Covenant enforces multi-factor authentication (MFA) and role-based access control (RBAC) ensuring that only authorized HR and operational managers can view individual employee records.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Typically, operational logs (like shift locations) are archived after a year, while core employment records are kept for the duration of employment plus mandated legal retention periods.

5. Your Rights & Contact

As an employee of Aegis Covenant, you have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections to inaccurate data, and in certain circumstances, request the deletion of non-essential records.
